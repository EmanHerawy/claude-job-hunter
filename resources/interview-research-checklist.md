# Interview Research Checklist

Extended research checklist covering leadership team, culture validation, and interviewer profiling. Use alongside the base `research-checklist.md` for company fundamentals.

Use WebSearch and WebFetch to research each area. Capture findings with source URLs for the COMPANY_INTEL.md output file.

## 1. Leadership Team Profiling

### CEO / Founder
- [ ] Background — previous companies, education, domain expertise
- [ ] Public statements — interviews, podcasts, conference talks, blog posts
- [ ] Leadership style signals — how they describe decision-making, culture
- [ ] Tenure — how long in role, previous tenure patterns
- [ ] Social media presence — Twitter/X, LinkedIn activity, tone

### CTO / VP Engineering
- [ ] Technical background — languages, systems, scale experience
- [ ] Engineering philosophy — blog posts, talks on tech culture, hiring
- [ ] Open source involvement
- [ ] Stated opinions on security, DevOps, infrastructure
- [ ] Previous companies — what kind of orgs did they build?

### Direct Manager (VP/Director over this role)
- [ ] LinkedIn profile — career trajectory, how long in current role
- [ ] Management style signals — any talks, posts, or interviews
- [ ] Team size they manage
- [ ] Previous roles — IC-turned-manager? Career manager? Technical?
- [ ] Mutual connections or shared background with candidate

### Suggested Searches
1. `"[NAME]" [COMPANY] interview OR podcast OR talk`
2. `"[NAME]" [COMPANY] leadership OR management OR culture`
3. `"[NAME]" linkedin`
4. `"[NAME]" site:youtube.com OR site:twitter.com`

## 2. Culture & Values Deep Dive

### Official Sources
- [ ] Company values page / leadership principles
- [ ] Careers page — how they describe culture
- [ ] About page — mission, vision statements
- [ ] Employee handbook or culture deck (if public)

### External Validation
- [ ] Glassdoor reviews — overall rating, CEO approval, "pros" and "cons" themes
- [ ] Glassdoor reviews filtered by role/department relevant to target position
- [ ] Blind posts — search for company name, look for recurring themes
- [ ] Indeed reviews — cross-reference with Glassdoor patterns
- [ ] Best-places-to-work awards or absence thereof

### Engineering Culture Signals
- [ ] Engineering blog — frequency, depth, topics (signals investment in eng brand)
- [ ] Open source projects — activity level, responsiveness to community
- [ ] Conference talks by engineers — what topics, what conferences
- [ ] Tech stack choices — modern vs. legacy, opinionated vs. pragmatic
- [ ] Developer experience investment — internal tooling, CI/CD, testing culture

### Red Flag Scanning
- [ ] High turnover in this specific role (check LinkedIn for predecessors, tenure)
- [ ] Negative review patterns — specific recurring complaints
- [ ] "Revolving door" signals — same role posted repeatedly
- [ ] Layoff history — recent or repeated layoffs
- [ ] Lawsuits or legal issues — employment disputes, discrimination claims
- [ ] Sudden leadership departures — CTO/VP turnover

### Suggested Searches
1. `"[COMPANY]" glassdoor reviews`
2. `"[COMPANY]" blind reviews`
3. `"[COMPANY]" engineering blog`
4. `"[COMPANY]" culture values`
5. `"[COMPANY]" layoffs OR turnover`
6. `"[COMPANY]" "[ROLE TITLE]" site:linkedin.com` (find predecessors)
7. `"[COMPANY]" open source github`

## 3. Hiring Team & Interview Process

### Likely Interviewers
- [ ] Hiring manager — identify from job posting, LinkedIn, or recruiter info
- [ ] Team leads — who runs the team this role sits on
- [ ] Cross-functional partners — eng leads, product, legal (depending on role)
- [ ] Skip-level — VP or C-level who might do final round

### For Each Identified Interviewer
- [ ] LinkedIn profile — background, tenure, career path
- [ ] Published content — talks, blog posts, tweets
- [ ] Technical interests and expertise areas
- [ ] Likely interview focus — what they'd evaluate
- [ ] Shared connections or background with candidate

### Interview Process Research
- [ ] Glassdoor interview reviews — reported process, difficulty, questions asked
- [ ] Company careers page — any description of hiring process
- [ ] Blog posts about their hiring philosophy
- [ ] Typical interview stages and timeline

### Suggested Searches
1. `"[COMPANY]" "[ROLE]" interview glassdoor`
2. `"[COMPANY]" hiring process`
3. `"[COMPANY]" "[ROLE]" site:linkedin.com`
4. `"[COMPANY]" interview questions`

---

## 4. Compensation & Benefits Context

- [ ] Levels.fyi or Glassdoor salary data for similar roles
- [ ] Funding stage and likely comp structure (cash vs. equity weighting)
- [ ] Equity type (options, RSUs, etc.) and vesting schedule if known
- [ ] Benefits highlighted on careers page
- [ ] Cost of living adjustment signals (remote vs. geo-adjusted)
- [ ] Recent fundraising — signals ability to pay competitively

### Suggested Searches
1. `"[COMPANY]" "[ROLE]" salary levels.fyi`
2. `"[COMPANY]" salary glassdoor`
3. `"[COMPANY]" benefits perks`
4. `"[COMPANY]" funding crunchbase`

---

## 5. Role-Specific Context

- [ ] How long has this role been open? (job posting date)
- [ ] Has this role been posted before? (indicates turnover or difficulty filling)
- [ ] Who held this role previously? (LinkedIn search for current/former employees)
- [ ] How long did predecessors stay?
- [ ] What does the team around this role look like? (adjacent roles, team size)
- [ ] Is this a new role (growth) or backfill (someone left)?
- [ ] Reporting structure — who does this role report to?

### Suggested Searches
1. `"[COMPANY]" "[ROLE]" site:linkedin.com`
2. `"[COMPANY]" "[ROLE]" job posting`
3. `"[COMPANY]" org chart OR team structure`
